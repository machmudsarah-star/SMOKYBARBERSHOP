Step By Step SEO Plan

Easy setup for Smoky Barbershop – complete each step and move to the next.

1

Setup & Tracking

Connect the site to the basic tools so you can see how many people visit and which searches bring them.

  • Create or log in to a Google account for the barbershop.
  • Set up Google Analytics for smokybarbershop.com and install the tracking code.
  • Set up Google Search Console and verify the website.
Open Google Analytics
Open Search Console
2

Website Basics

Make the homepage clear for Google and customers using simple text and titles.

  • Set the page title to something like: “Smoky Barbershop | Skin Fades & Men’s Haircuts in Bowen Hills”.
  • Add a short description on the homepage explaining who you are, what you do, and where you are located.
  • Make sure the site looks good on a phone (text big enough, buttons easy to tap).
3

Service Pages

Each main service should have its own short page that Google can find.

  • Create or edit pages for: Classic Cut, Skin Fade, Beard Trim, Long Hair, etc.
  • Add 1–2 short paragraphs that describe the service and mention Bowen Hills / Brisbane.
  • Add a clear “Book now” button that links to your booking page.
Open Booking Page
4

Google Maps & Reviews

Optimise your Google Business Profile so you show up for “barber near me”.

  • Claim or log in to your Google Business Profile for Smoky Barbershop.
  • Check that the name, address, phone number and opening hours match your website.
  • Ask happy clients to leave a review and reply to every review with a quick thank you.
Open Google Business Profile
5

Content & Photos

Use photos and short posts to show your work and keep the site active.

  • Add recent haircut photos to a gallery page and to Google Business Profile.
  • Once a month, write a short “tips” post (for example: how often to get a fade).
  • Share the post on Instagram and add a link to your website in your bio.
6

Ongoing Checkups

Every few months, check the numbers and make small improvements.

  • Look in Google Analytics to see which pages people visit most.
  • Check Google Search Console for which search words show your site.
  • Update your most important pages once or twice a year with fresh text and photos.
